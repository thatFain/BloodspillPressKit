Description

Bloodspill is a fast-paced, pvp retro fps with one hit combat, featuring classic game modes like Deathmatch, Capture the Flag and Conquest.

Deathmatch

Kill or be killed! The first player to fulfill the body count wins.

Capture the Flag

Get that flag! Steal the flag from the enemy team and capture it at your own base to gain points and bring victory to your team.

Conquest

Conquer and Defend! Hold your conquered territory and pile up points to win the round!
